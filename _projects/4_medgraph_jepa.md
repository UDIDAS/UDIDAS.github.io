**MedGraph-JEPA** is an ongoing project exploring **joint-embedding predictive architectures** applied to multimodal medical knowledge graphs — combining EHR, imaging, and genomic modalities.

### Scope

The project builds on MIMIC-IV-MM (12,000+ tri-modal patients) and CPTAC oncology cohorts to learn structured representations that capture cross-modal relationships without reliance on generative pretraining objectives.

### Status

- Literature review and dataset selection locked
- PhysioNet credentialing and dbGaP applications in progress
- Targeting **NeurIPS 2026 Datasets & Benchmarks track**

More updates coming soon.
